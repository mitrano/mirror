## Texto base

### Instrução

**ANSWER QUESTIONS 01, 02 AND 03 IN ENGLISH BASED ON THE TEXT BELOW.**

### Text I — Joe's routine

> I wake up at seven o'clock on weekdays. I brush my teeth, get dressed and have breakfast. I usually eat eggs, honey, butter, cheese, tomatoes and orange juice. I leave home at eight and go to school by bus. My classes run from half past eight to three o'clock. I have lunch at school with my friends.
>
> After school, I go to my music course and play the violin. I get home around five, watch cartoons, do my homework and have dinner with my family at eight. After dinner, I listen to music, sometimes search online for school information, read a book and go to bed.
>
> On Saturdays, my family and I usually go shopping at the supermarket. Sometimes we watch a movie at the cinema. In the afternoon, we walk in the park and feed the birds.
>
> On Sundays, we visit my grandparents in their village. I play with their dog and help milk the cows. Sometimes I pick fruit or vegetables from their garden. My grandmother makes strawberry cake and serves it with tea. We sit in the living room, talk and sometimes play games. In the afternoon, we return home around four o'clock.
