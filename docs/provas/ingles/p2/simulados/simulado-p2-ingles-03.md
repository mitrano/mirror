Last Friday, Daniel and his classmates went on a school trip to a museum. They took the bus at seven o'clock and got on it in front of the school. Daniel sat next to his best friend, Leo, and they talked about their favourite outdoor activities.

In the afternoon, the students got off the bus at school. Daniel came home by car with his father. At home, he wrote a paragraph about the trip and chose a picture for his notebook.

# GABARITO COMENTADO

## Questão 01

Resposta esperada:

a. Daniel and his classmates went to a museum last Friday.  
b. He wrote a paragraph about the trip and chose a picture for his notebook.

Comentário:
O aluno deve localizar informações explícitas no texto e responder com frase completa em inglês.

## Questão 02

Resposta esperada:

Daniel and his classmates took the bus to the museum. / They went to the museum by bus.

Comentário:
A resposta deve indicar corretamente o transporte usado e estar em frase completa.

## Questão 03

Resposta esperada:

T — T — F — T

Comentário:
A terceira frase é falsa porque o texto menciona teddy bear, skipping rope e painting, mas não merry-go-round.

## Questão 04

Resposta esperada:

B. slide — hopscotch — skipping rope

Comentário:
Essas são atividades ou brinquedos normalmente associados a ambientes abertos. As outras alternativas misturam itens indoor.

## Questão 05

Resposta esperada:

a. taught  
b. got  
c. took  
d. came  
e. saw  
f. chose

Comentário:
A questão cobra Simple Past de verbos irregulares importantes da revisão.

## Questão 06

Resposta esperada:

a. Negative: He didn't write a paragraph about the trip.  
a. Interrogative: Did he write a paragraph about the trip?

b. Negative: They didn't have lunch in the park.  
b. Interrogative: Did they have lunch in the park?

Comentário:
Depois de **did/didn't**, o verbo principal volta para a forma base: write, have.

## Questão 07

Resposta esperada:

a. on the bus  
b. in the car  
c. take the bus  
d. get on  
e. get off  
f. ride a bike

Comentário:
A questão avalia collocations de transporte: on para transportes grandes, in para car, get on para embarcar e get off para descer.

## Questão 08

Resposta esperada:

a. got on  
b. in  
c. take  
d. write  
e. rode

Comentário:
A questão mistura collocations de transporte e Simple Past. Depois de did/didn't, usa-se forma base.

## Questão 09

Resposta esperada:

a. She didn't write about the trip.  
b. Did he see a teddy bear at the museum?  
c. They got on the plane at 9 a.m.

Comentário:
Os erros principais são o uso do passado depois de did/didn't e a collocation incorreta com plane. Para plane, usa-se get on/got on.
