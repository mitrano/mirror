# CV20.DS6.US5 — Close A Refinement Story

**Type:** User Story  
**Status:** 🟡 Planned

## Story

As Navigator, I want to review, coherence-check, and close a Refinement Story so
that the full refinement arc records what changed, what was parked, what was
promoted, and what follow-up remains.

## Outcome

Builder renders RS-level review, coherence, and close surfaces. The RS closes
only when accepted CRs have outcomes, validation evidence is preserved, and
coherence passes.

## Notes

Review may feed debt or follow-up records, but it does not perform refactoring
outside a CR cycle.
