# CV18.DS2 — Beauty Voice

**Status:** ✅ Done

**Placement:** Second new voice in `v0.25.0 — Soul Mode More Voices`

**User-visible outcome:** A user can ask to hear Beauty Voice during Soul Mode and receive a coherent ritual listening card.

---

## Why This Exists

Soul Mode should not only listen for center, shadow, and wisdom. It also needs a lens for beauty: the form of aliveness still present inside a situation.

Beauty Voice is not decoration, reassurance, or shallow positivity. It listens for form, delicacy, texture, care, literature, poetry, image, rhythm, atmosphere, and the place where life still breathes even when the user's material is tense. The voice appears inside the card below `the voice says`; Mirror's bridge to the user's concrete material happens outside the card.

---

## Scope

- Add Beauty Voice as an active Soul Mode listening lens.
- Include Beauty Voice in Possible Listenings when living matter is sufficient.
- Render Beauty Voice with the same ritual grammar as Self, Shadow, and Wisdom.
- Add the prompt and command/service behavior needed for the user to hear the voice.
- Preserve the rule that voice content appears inside the card under `the voice says`.
- Keep Mirror's bridge outside the voice card.
- Ensure Beauty Voice does not mutate identity, journal, journey state, or project files.

---

## Non-goals

- No beauty fragment or poetry library.
- No aesthetic recommendation engine.
- No forced optimism.
- No closing integration rite.
- No psyche-layer enrichment.
- No identity mutation.
- No rich UI.

---

## Acceptance Behavior

Given Soul Mode has enough living matter, Possible Listenings includes Beauty Voice as an available listening.

Given the user asks to hear Beauty Voice, Mirror renders a Beauty Voice listening card with the voice response under `the voice says`.

Given Beauty Voice responds, the card reveals form, aliveness, delicacy, texture, image, rhythm, or care already present in the material, omits `listening for`, and does not try to make the situation positive.

Given Beauty Voice runs, no journal entry, identity entry, or project-state mutation is created automatically.
